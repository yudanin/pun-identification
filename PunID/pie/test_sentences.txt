# Example sentences for PIE testing
# Each line is a sentence to analyze

# Homophonic puns
Atheism is a non-prophet institution.
I used to be a banker, but I lost interest.
When the cannibal showed up late to the party, they gave him the cold shoulder.

# Homographic puns
My shoe is a foot long.
Time flies like an arrow; fruit flies like a banana.
I'm reading a book about anti-gravity. It's impossible to put down.

# Recursive puns
Immanuel doesn't pun, he Kant.
A Freudian slip is when you say one thing and mean your mother.

# Antanaclasis
We must, indeed, all hang together or, most assuredly, we shall all hang separately.
If you aren't fired with enthusiasm, you will be fired with enthusiasm.
Your argument is sound, nothing but sound.

# No pun (control)
The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
I went to the store to buy some groceries.
